Been a rough few weeks, correcting 100 ADA stories. How will you celebrate? A few drinks? A holiday maybe? Why not both :-) Welcome to BTA (Beer Trip Agency SA), where we design beer-tasting trips around the world taking into account our customers' preferences! Using the provided data, we design several trips around the world, taking into account different parameters that might be relevant to our drunk-driven clients. We pick the best spots corresponding to characteristics such as alcohol percentage, top-rated beers, and brewery diversity among other things. Then, we establish a general ranking designed in relation to customer preferences, giving more or less weight to the initial ratings. Once our algorithm brews through, we determine the regions that will make up the world tour. We'll propose brewery visits in countries, along with the contacts of the region's best beer lovers/experts. Ale the best for a trip Lager than life!

1. A Couple's ABV (Alcohol by Volume) Adventure: Harmonizing Contrasts Meet Beatriz and Bob, a couple who share a love of travel and a passion for beer. However, their beer preferences couldn't be more different. Beatriz, drawn to bold, intense flavors, dreams of exploring the world with ABV Adventure: The Bold Bataillon. As for Bob, who has a taste for subtlety and finesse, he envisions a journey with ABV Adventure: The Soft Symphony.

1.1 ABV Adventure: The Soft Symphony Welcome to The Soft Symphony, an enchanting journey through countries known for their nuanced beers with lower alcohol percentages. Imagine walking through the bustling streets of Trinidad and Tobago, where the gentle melodies of local beers dance on your palate. Bosnia-Herzegovina, with its rich cultural tapestry, offers a serenade of subtle flavors, while Albania's beers are a symphony of finesse. Exploring the softer side of beer in destinations like Jersey and Lebanon, you'll find yourself immersed in a world where each sip is a delicate note in a harmonious composition.

The Sweet Symphony is not just a tour, it's a sensory experience, an exploration of countries that have mastered the art of brewing with elegance.

1.2 ABV Adventure: The Bold Battalion Walk alongside the Bataillon des audacieux, an adventurous tour designed for those who want to be bold and robust. Imagine yourself in the heart of Belgium, surrounded by the imposing presence of Trappist beers and strong, tasty ales. Estonia, the hidden gem of the beer world, offers a battalion of craft beers that don't lack punch. Egypt, with its ancient brewing traditions, unleashes a battalion of flavors that will captivate your taste buds.

Crossing the battlefields of Iceland and the Netherlands, every sip becomes a bold statement. The Bold Battalion isn't just a tour, it's a conquest of countries where beer is a force to be reckoned with. From Rhode Island's bold IPAs to the Netherlands, each destination is a bastion of the strongest, creating a symphony of intensity for bold palates.
